How often should I schedule maintenance for my garage door in Dallas and how to know that my garage door needs it




  • Routine Inspection

    Check for wear damage or misalignment by visually inspecting your garage door at least once every few months.


  • Lubrication

    Apply lubricant to moving parts such as rollers, hinges and springs every six months to ensure smooth operation and prevent premature wear.


  • Balance Test

    Every year, disconnect your garage door operator and physically raise the entry halfway to perform a balancing test on your entrance. If it doesn't stay in place, it may be out of balance and require adjustment.


  • Safety Sensor Test

    When the door is closing, test the security sensors by waving an object in front of them. The sensors may require adjustment or substitute if the gate does not reverse immediately.


  • Spring Tension

    Check the tension of the garage door springs periodically by visually inspecting them for signs of wear or stretching. Springs that appear worn or damaged should be replaced promptly.


  • Opener Inspection

    Test the garage door opener by opening and closing the entry several times, listening for unusual noises or vibrations. Any signs of malfunction may indicate the need for maintenance or fix.


  • Professional Tune Up

    To make sure all the parts are in good shape and are operating as intended schedule an expert door maintenance service from our company at least once a year. knowledgeable experts can detect problems early on and take care of them to prolong the life of your garage gate and prevent expensive repairs.
