What are the best garage door openers for homes in Dallas TX

Chain Drive Garage Door Opener

This type of opener uses a metal chain to operate the entry. It's the most common and economical option but tends to be noisy during operation.

Belt Operate Garage Gate Mechanism

Similar to the chain drive opener, but instead of a chain, it uses a reinforced rubber belt to move the door. Belt drive openers are quieter than chain drive openers and are a popular choice for homes with attached garages.

Screw Automatic Garage Door Mechanism

With screw drive openers, the entrance door can be raised or decreased using a threaded steel rod. They require less maintenance than chain or belt drive openers and are suitable for most residential garage doors.

Direct Operate Garage Entry Mechanism

These types which are mounted to the wall next to the garage door, are also called jackshaft openers. We use a motor to turn a shaft, which in turn moves the door. Direct drive openers are quiet, compact, and ideal for garages with limited headroom.

Smart WiFi Enabled Garage Door Opener

These modern openers can be controlled remotely using a smartphone app. They often come with additional features such as real time alerts, scheduling and integration with smart home systems.

Battery Backup

These openers come equipped with a battery backup system that allows the door to operate during power outages. They supply added convenience and security especially in areas prone to power failures.

Commercial Garage Door Opener

Designed for heavier and more frequent use, commercial garage door openers are built to withstand rigorous conditions. They often feature higher horsepower motors and are capable of lifting larger and more heavy doors.

How do I troubleshoot common issues with your garage door opener in Dallas TX




  • Check Power Source

    Make sure the garage entrance opener is powered on and plugged in. Check the fuse box or circuit breaker for any blown fuses or tripped breakers.


  • Inspect Remote Batteries

    If the remote control isn't working, replace the batteries and ensure they are properly inserted. Test the remote to see if it now operates the garage door opener.


  • Verify Safety Sensors

    Make sure the security sensors located near the bottom of the garage door tracks are aligned properly and free from obstructions. Clear away any debris or dirt that might be obscuring the sensors.


  • Test Wall Switch

    Verify that the wall switch has been properly attached to the opener unit if it isn't working. Look for any damage or loose connections in the cabling. If required, think about having a new switch.


  • Lubricate Moving Parts

    To ensure flawless operation, lubricate the tracks, rollers, and hinges. Check that all the screws and bolts on the garage entrance opener unit are tight.


  • Reset Opener

    After a few minutes of being disconnected from the power source and then replunged, attempt to reset the garage door opener. If the issue still exists Test the opener to see if the issue persists.


  • Call Professional Help

    If troubleshooting steps fail to resolve the issue contact our company Alco Garage Door Dallas TX for expert assistance. Our trained technicians can diagnose and repair any complex problems with your garage entrance opener efficiently and effectively.
